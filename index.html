Welcome to Resala STEM

We are here to help everyone in need

We represent a charitable team dedicated to providing high school students with the opportunity to volunteer with prominent charity organizations. Established in 2021 as an extension of the esteemed Resala Organization, our initiative initially began as a single branch. Since then, we have expanded our presence across Egypt, boasting over 75 branches throughout the country.
