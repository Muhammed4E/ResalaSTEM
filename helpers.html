Resala STEM Helpers

Resala STEM Helpers is a subteam of Resala STEM, dedicated to spreading knowledge for anyone in need. Resala STEM Helpers' activities include meetings with alumni about summer programs, scholarships, and studying tips. In addition, we produce videos related to the Resala STEM team, showcasing contributions to competitions and other endeavors.
